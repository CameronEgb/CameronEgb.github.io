Research Interests

Neurosymbolic Reinforcement Learning

My research focuses on empowering successful connectionist approaches with logically verifiable and explainable symbolic methods. By integrating neural networks with symbolic planning and reasoning, I aim to create AI systems that combine the learning capabilities of deep learning with the interpretability and reliability of symbolic AI. This hybrid approach promises to deliver more trustworthy, transparent, and robust intelligent systems that can both learn from data and reason about their decisions in human-understandable terms.

Research Methodologies

My work employs a diverse set of methodologies including:

  • Deep Reinforcement Learning (DRL) with symbolic constraints
  • Logic-based program synthesis and inductive logic programming
  • Hierarchical planning with neural sub-policies
  • Knowledge graph integration with neural architectures
  • Formal verification methods for learned policies
